Detailed Description

Implements the landmark motion factor that models the displacement of a tracked point, i, on a rigid body, j between consequative object poses ^wL_{k-1} and ^wL_k.

Cost residual is implemented as: ^wm_k - ^wL_k ^wL_{k-1}^{-1} ^wm_{k-1}
