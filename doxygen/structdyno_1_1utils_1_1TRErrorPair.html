Calculates the relative pose error between poses.

First calcualte the relative pose change between the reference and current frames ie. ref_T_curr and ref_T_hat_curr and then take the pose error between these two relative poses.

The ref frame is usually the previous frame when analysing frame to frame motion but is just used to calcualte the relative transformation
