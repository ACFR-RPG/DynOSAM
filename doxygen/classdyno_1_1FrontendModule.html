protectedpure virtual

Checks that the incoming ImageContainer meeets the minimum requirement for the derived module.

This is specifically to check that the right image types are contained with the container. If the result is false, InvalidImageContainerException is thrown with the requirements specified by the returned ImageValidationResult.
