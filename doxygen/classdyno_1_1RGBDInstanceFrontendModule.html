const Frame::Ptr &  frame_k_1

private

Solves PnP between frame_k-1 and frame_k using the tracked correspondances to estimate the frame of the current camera.

the pose of the Frame::Ptr (frame_k) is updated, and the features marked as outliers by PnP are set as outliers.
