Detailed Description

We divide the image into a grid of cells and try to find maximally one feature per cell. This is to ensure good distribution of features in the image. https://github.com/uzh-rpg/rpg_svo_pro_open/blob/master/svo_common/include/svo/common/occupancy_grid_2d.h
